Downsides to Phone Gap

The Nuts and Bolts

While Phone Gap is an incrediible advanced and sophisticated piece o software, there ae as with all good things some drawbacks

Immersion
Native languages, such as swift for ios, were created with tghe explict goal of feeling comfortable on the platform for which they were made. As such, when useing Phone Gap, some aesthetic and sensory aspects of the user experince may endup being sacrificed.

Speed
Native, platform specific languages are often created to streamline certain processes. Therfore, when using Phone Gap, certain opperations may be substantially slower, especially as the mobile application gets more complex.

APIs
Phone Gap requires numerous APIs and plugins to interact with the various physical aspects of a mobile device. Native languages have both easier and more efficent waysof comunicating with certain pieces of hardware.

Graphics
While JS/CSS graphics can be used to simulate or display most data sets and models, most native mobile devolpment languages have special and powerful graphics models to dislay vissual images

Support
Not all aspects of a mobile device are supported by Phone Gap or one of the many APIs and plugins. As such, native devolpment languages are particularilly adept at dealing with compilcated hardware.

However ...

Despite all the potential draw backs, Phone Gap allows for devolpers to create projects easilly, quicklly turn an idea into a working prototype, and then use the Phone Gap app to scale up a project. Often Phone Gap is used to prototype projects.
